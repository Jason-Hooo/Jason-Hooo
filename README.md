## 👨‍💻 About Me

* 🎓 **Undergraduate at NCCU:** Double majoring in Management Information Systems and Artificial Intelligence Applications.
* 💼 **AI Engineer Intern:** Led a 6-member team at ISRC (NCCU) to develop "Yuan-Bao", an Agentic RAG chatbot from scratch, providing Indigenous students at NCCU with 24/7 access to essential information.
* 🎯 **Career Goal:** Actively seeking an **AI Engineer Internship** to apply generative AI and machine learning to real-world products.
* 💡 **Current Passion:** Bridging the gap between frontend and AI — currently exploring the integration of **SwiftUI** with robust **AI backends** to build seamless, AI-driven mobile experiences.
* 🏁 **Upcoming:** Joining in the [DevJam TW 2026](https://devjam.tw/) Hackathon on **August 17–18, 2026**!
